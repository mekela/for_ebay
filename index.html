About this item:

Each order is limited to 15 characters. Need more? No problem! Just order another item (2 items = 30 characters, 3 items = 45 characters, etc). Need a different size? Again, no problem! We cut vinyl up to 20 inches high and as long as needed. Use the links on the left to find our other heights and products.

The size of your vinyl lettering decal will depend upon the lettering height you purchase and the length of your text. Please note letter height is measured from the highest to lowest points of your text. This will include the lower portion of letters that go beneath the base of a capital letters (such as letters g,p,y etc). For example the height of the name "Tony" would be measured from the top of the "T" to the lower tip of the "y."

Please note that longer text orders may be split into two decals. Of course, we will only split the decal in between words. You may still easily apply these two decals next to each other on your wall for a single longer decal.

About the Vinyl:

We use high quality Oracal 631 vinyl! All indoor colors have a matte finish and are removable. The metallic colors are also Oracal 631 and are removable. The manufacturer claims a 3 year indoor life with intact removal for 2 years. These decals are easy to apply to walls, doors, windows, mirrors, metal, painted wood or any other solid, smooth or lightly textured surface. Please note the surface should be clean and dry prior to installing. Please note, applying on newly painted walls, less than 2 weeks old, is not recommended. Applying to walls with chalk like (dusty) finish is also not recommended. The exact lifespan of your decal will depend upon its usage and exposure. Many decals last longer than 3 years.
